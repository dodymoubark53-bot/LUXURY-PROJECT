1 program | CLASSIC JORDANIA (8 days / 7 nights)
DEPARTURES (SATURDAY / SUNDAY / TUESDAY / WEDNESDAY / THURSDAY)
*The order of the program changes depending on the day of arrival (See the weekly order of arrivals)
Day 1 AIRPORT / AMMAN
Departure on a regular flight to Amman. Arrival and transfer to the hotel of chosen category. Dinner (provided arrival at the hotel is before 9:00 PM). Accommodation.

Day 2 AMMAN / DEAD SEA / AMMAN
Breakfast. Departure to the Dead Sea, descending to nearly 400 meters below sea level, where we can enjoy a swim. After the visit, return to Amman, free afternoon. Dinner and accommodation at the hotel.

Day 3 AMMAN / MADABA / MOUNT NEBO / SHOBAK PANORAMIC / PETRA
Breakfast and departure to Madaba. Arrival and visit of St. George's Church, with its famous mosaic depicting all the biblical territories, where the first mosaic map of Palestine is found. Continue to Mount Nebo, from whose summit Moses saw the promised land, which he would never reach. Continue to Shobak Castle, a solitary reminder of the ancient glory of the Crusaders. Continue to Petra. Arrival in Petra. Dinner and accommodation at the hotel.

Day 4 PETRA VISIT
Breakfast. Full day dedicated to visiting Petra, known as the "Rose City", where more than 2,000 years ago the Nabataeans established the capital of their empire for over 500 years, carving admirable temples and tombs into the rose-colored mountains and using advanced agricultural and water distribution systems. The tour begins at the Tomb of the Obelisks, continuing through the Siq, a canyon over 1 km long, at the end of which the Treasury is revealed, a colossal tomb decorated with columns and sculptures of unparalleled refinement and beauty. Continue to the Street of Facades and the theater, then approach the 850 steps that will take us to the imposing Monastery "El Deir". After the visit, return to the hotel. Dinner and accommodation.

Day 5 PETRA / LITTLE PETRA / WADI RUM (2HR 4X4) / AMMAN
Breakfast. Excursion to the Wadi Rum desert and 4x4 vehicle ride. We will venture into the pink sands of this desert, which has a special charm provided by the granite rock formations that nature has shaped into whimsical forms. Return to Amman. Rest of the day free at the hotel. Dinner and accommodation.

Day 6 AMMAN FREE DAY
Breakfast. Free day in Amman. Dinner and accommodation at the hotel.

Day 7 AMMAN / AMMAN CITY TOUR / JERASH / AJLUN / AMMAN
Next, we will take a tour of Amman, which includes the Citadel, the Archaeological Museum, and the Roman Theater. Afterwards, we will visit Jerash or "Gerasa", a Greco-Roman city that was part of the Decapolis and is known as the "Pompeii of the East" for its importance and magnificent state of preservation. Among other things, we can admire: Hadrian's Arch, the Hippodrome, the Theater, the Agora or forum with its complete colonnade, the Cardo Maximus, the Temple of Zeus, and the Temple of Artemis. Then we will visit Ajlun Castle, a fortress built in 1185 and later rebuilt in the 13th century by the Mamluks after its destruction by the Mongols. It is a castle from the Crusader period, located high on the mountain, offering a beautiful view. Return to Amman. Dinner and accommodation.

Day 8 AMMAN / AMMAN AIRPORT
Breakfast and transfer to the airport. Departure on a return flight to the city of origin. Arrival and end of the trip.
